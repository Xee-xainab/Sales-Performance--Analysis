# Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes sales performance data using Microsoft Excel to provide insights into revenue generation, product performance, branch performance, and order status trends. An interactive dashboard was developed to help stakeholders monitor key business metrics and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

Businesses need to understand which branches and products contribute the most to revenue while identifying operational challenges that may impact growth. This dashboard was created to answer key business questions related to sales performance and customer orders.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance.
* Identify top-performing branches.
* Determine best-selling products.
* Track monthly revenue trends.
* Evaluate order fulfillment status.
* Generate actionable business recommendations.

---

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Pivot Charts
* Slicers
* Dashboard Design and Visualization

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Revenue: ₦1,156,239
* Total Orders: 100
* Total Quantity Sold: 1,075

---

## Key Findings

### Branch Performance

* Kano generated the highest revenue (₦463,706).
* Lagos ranked second with ₦366,509.
* Abuja recorded the lowest revenue (₦326,024).

### Product Performance

* Watches and Shoes were the highest-performing products based on order volume.
* Product demand was concentrated in a few key categories.

### Revenue Trend

* Revenue peaked in January.
* Revenue declined steadily throughout the year.
* December recorded the lowest sales performance.

### Order Status

* Cancelled and pending orders exceeded shipped orders.
* This indicates opportunities to improve order fulfillment processes.

---

## Recommendations

1. Increase marketing efforts in Abuja to improve branch performance.
2. Maintain adequate stock levels for high-demand products.
3. Investigate causes of order cancellations and implement corrective measures.
4. Introduce seasonal promotions to boost end-of-year sales.
5. Replicate successful sales strategies from Kano across other branches.

---

## Conclusion

The analysis shows that Kano is the strongest-performing branch while Watches remain one of the most demanded product categories. Although the business generated over ₦1.15 million in revenue, declining monthly sales and high cancellation rates highlight areas for improvement. Implementing the recommended actions can help increase revenue, improve customer satisfaction, and drive sustainable business growth.
